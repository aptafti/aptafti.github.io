AI Consulting Services

With extensive experience in AI-powered healthcare informatics and a profound commitment to advancing patient care through cutting-edge AI technologies, I offer specialized consulting services in artificial intelligence (AI). My background and expertise in AI, including my BSc, MSc, and PhD in Computer Science, a robust portfolio of over 60 peer-reviewed publications and my long-standing research accomplishments in AI demonstrate my solid expertise and skill sets in AI, machine learning, computer vision, and medical image analysis. My consulting services are tailored to harness the potential of AI in healthcare, focusing on improving patient diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment through sophisticated computational algorithms and diverse biomedical data sources. With that, I bring a deep understanding of both the theoretical and practical aspects of AI applications in health informatics.

Whether you need guidance on integrating AI technologies into healthcare systems or developing, building, training, or validating innovative AI-powered solutions for complex health data challenges, I am dedicated to delivering impactful and actionable insights to drive your projects -fast and bold- forward.

The initial 30 minutes of AI consultation are offered at no charge. Following this complimentary session, we will discuss and determine the applicable fees based on the scope and requirements of the consulting engagement. For inquiries or to schedule a consultation, please reach out via email to tafti@ieee.org with the subject line "AI Consulting Services".
